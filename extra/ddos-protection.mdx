---

<Steps>
    <Step title="Register for Cloudflare">
        Head over to <a href="https://www.cloudflare.com/" target="_blank">Cloudflare</a> and sign up.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Site">
        Add your site to Cloudflare
    </Step>
    <Step title="Point to Cloudflare's nameservers">
        Head over to your domain registar's admin panel, setup a Custom DNS and add Cloudflare's nameservers.
    </Step>
</Steps>
